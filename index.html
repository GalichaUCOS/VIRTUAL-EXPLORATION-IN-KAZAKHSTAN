Welcome to Kazakhstan

Explore the rich culture, breathtaking landscapes, and unique traditions of Kazakhstan.

3 Facts about Kazakhstan

🚀 Space Legacy

Kazakhstan is home to the Baikonur Cosmodrome, the world's first and largest space launch facility, from where Yuri Gagarin, the first human in space, launched in 1961.

🏜️ Vast Landscape

Kazakhstan is the world's largest landlocked country, covering over 2.7 million square kilometers, featuring everything from mountains to deserts and vast steppe landscapes.

🐎 Nomadic Traditions

The country has a deep-rooted nomadic culture, with traditions such as horse riding, eagle hunting, and the famous dish Beshbarmak, which means "five fingers."

Leisure Activities in Kazakhstan

⛷️ Skiing & Snowboarding

Shymbulak Ski Resort, located in the picturesque mountains near Almaty, offers fantastic skiing and snowboarding opportunities with breathtaking alpine views.

🏕️ Camping & Hiking

The diverse landscapes of Kazakhstan make it perfect for outdoor adventures, from hiking in the Tian Shan mountains to camping in the vast steppe regions.

🐎 Horseback Riding

Experience Kazakhstan’s nomadic heritage by exploring the steppe on horseback, just like the ancient Kazakh people did for centuries.

🌊 Boating on the Caspian Sea

Relax on the shores of the Caspian Sea, enjoy boat rides, and explore the unique coastal environment of the world’s largest inland body of water.
