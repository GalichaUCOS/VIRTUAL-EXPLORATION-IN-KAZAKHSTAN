Must-Visit Places in Kazakhstan

Discover the breathtaking landmarks and hidden gems of Kazakhstan.

Top 5 Places to Visit

🏔️ Charyn Canyon

Often called the "Grand Canyon of Kazakhstan," Charyn Canyon is a stunning natural wonder with dramatic rock formations and scenic hiking trails.

🏛️ Astana (Nur-Sultan)

The modern capital city boasts incredible architecture, including the Bayterek Tower and Khan Shatyr, along with rich cultural experiences.

🌊 Caspian Sea Coast

Experience the vast beauty of the Caspian Sea, where you can enjoy beach resorts, boat tours, and fresh seafood.

🏞️ Kolsai Lakes

Located in the Tien Shan mountains, these pristine alpine lakes offer serene landscapes, excellent trekking, and a peaceful getaway.

🕌 Mausoleum of Khoja Ahmed Yasawi

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, this ancient mausoleum in Turkestan is a significant religious and historical landmark.
